# 🕌 Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and respectful community guided by Islamic principles and values.

---

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment aligned with Islamic teachings, we pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, or religion. We uphold the Islamic principles of justice, compassion, and mutual respect.

---

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language that respects Islamic values
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences while maintaining modesty and decency
- Offering constructive criticism with wisdom and good advice (as encouraged in Islamic teachings)
- Focusing on what’s best for the community and collective benefit
- Demonstrating patience, forgiveness, and kindness in interactions

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or slander (ghibah)
- Public or private intimidation, threats, or coercion
- Publishing others’ private information without permission
- Using obscene, vulgar, or indecent language
- Promoting or engaging in behavior that contradicts Islamic moral standards
- Disrespecting religious beliefs or practices
- Any form of inappropriate gender interactions that violate Islamic guidelines for modesty
- Any other behavior deemed inappropriate according to Islamic principles

---

## Islamic Guidelines
- Maintain modesty (haya) in speech and conduct
- Respect the boundaries set by Islamic teachings regarding interactions between genders
- Avoid backbiting, gossip, and spreading rumors
- Promote positive contributions that benefit the Muslim community and society
- Show compassion and support for fellow community members

---

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via GitHub Issues or private contact.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Decisions will be made in accordance with Islamic principles of justice and fairness.
