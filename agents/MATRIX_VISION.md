## 💰 THE REVENUE MODEL

**How We Make Money (and stay fair):**

### Commission Structure
- **Delivery fee:** Customer pays (e.g., 10 EGP)
- **Commission:** We keep 10% of delivery fee (1 EGP)
- **Courier gets:** 90% of delivery fee (9 EGP)
- **Vendor pays:** Small fee for order exposure

### Example Delivery
```
Customer orders coffee delivery:
- Coffee cost: 50 EGP
- Delivery fee: 10 EGP (customer choice)
- Total: 60 EGP

Matrix takes: 10% commission = 1 EGP
Courier gets: 9 EGP
Vendor gets: 50 EGP
```

### Other Revenue (Later)
- **Premium features:** Verified vendors, priority delivery
- **Sponsored listings:** Vendors can pay for visibility
- **Referral bonuses:** We match 10% bonus for recruiter (from commission)
- **Insurance:** Courier protection (optional, small fee)
- **Merchant tools:** Analytics, management dashboard

### Why This Works
- Couriers earn 9x more than competitors (who take 30%)
- Vendors attract customers (faster, cheaper delivery)
- Customers get transparent pricing
- We stay profitable at scale
